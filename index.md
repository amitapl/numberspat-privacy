*Last updated: June 24, 2026*

Deim Studios ("we", "us") operates the **Numbers Pat** mobile game (the "App"). This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

- **Firebase Analytics** (Google) — anonymized usage and event data: screens viewed, levels played, session length, device model, OS version, country, and a randomly generated app-instance ID.
- **Firebase Crashlytics** (Google) — crash reports: stack traces, device state at time of crash, OS version, and the app-instance ID. Used only to diagnose and fix crashes.

We do **not** collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, location, or audio recordings. The App does not require an account or login.

## Data Stored Only On Your Device

Game progress, level records, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us.

## How We Use Data

- Improve gameplay, difficulty balancing, and stability.
- Diagnose and fix crashes and bugs.
- Understand aggregate usage trends.

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

## Third-Party Services

Data handled by Google Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:  
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)  
Firebase data handling: [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Analytics data is anonymized. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

## Data Retention

Analytics and crash data are retained per Google Firebase default retention settings. You can reset your app-instance identifier by reinstalling the App.

## Your Choices

- Uninstall the App to stop all data collection.
- Reinstall to reset the anonymous app-instance ID.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
