Frequently Asked Questions

My progress was lost. Can I recover it?
Progress is stored locally on your device. Reinstalling the app or restoring from a backup may reset it. Unfortunately we cannot recover lost progress remotely.

The app crashed. What should I do?
Force-close the app and reopen it. If crashes persist, try reinstalling. You can also email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll investigate.

A level seems impossible or broken.
Email us with the level number and a description of what's happening. We'll look into it for the next update.

How do I reset all my progress?
Reinstalling the app clears all saved data.
